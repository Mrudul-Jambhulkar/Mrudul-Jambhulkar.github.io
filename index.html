About Me

Hey! I am Mrudul Jambhulkar, pursuing a Dual Degree (B.Tech. + M.Tech.) in Electrical Engineering with a specialization in Communication & Signal Processing at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. My interests span machine learning, VLSI design, computer architecture, signal processing, and their intersections in real-world applications. As part of my dual degree project, I am currently working on machine learning techniques for image segmentation, knowledge distillation, and hyperspectral image analysis, with a particular focus on applications in agriculture and satellite imagery.

In my free time, I enjoy playing chess and listening to music.

Autonomous Defect Estimation in Aluminum Plate and Prognosis Through Stochastic Process Modeling

Mrudul Jambhulkar, Shivam Ojha, Amit Shelke, Anowarul Habib

This work presents an autonomous framework for defect estimation and prognosis in aluminum plates using advanced signal processing and stochastic modeling, achieving a MAPE of 9.9% in damage progression predictions.
